Welcome to Your AI-Powered Crypto Prediction Assistant

This is your AI-powered crypto prediction assistant, designed to give you live, accurate, and evolving trading forecasts directly inside the app. Powered by cutting-edge AI models including LSTM, Transformers, FinBERT for sentiment analysis, and reinforcement learning (PPO), this system doesn’t just predict — it learns from its past and grows smarter with every trade scenario it sees.

  • API Keys: Enter your keys for CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, or CryptoCompare for live prices. For news, use NewsAPI and/or CryptoPanic.
  • Supported Coins: BTC/USDT and ETH/USDT are recommended for best results. You can expand to others later.
  • Sentiment Analysis: The AI reads market moods in real time from news sources, giving you an edge on big headlines and sentiment shifts.
  • Customization: Choose your timeframe, indicators (RSI, EMA, MACD, Bollinger Bands), and risk controls (capital at risk, SL/TP, etc.).
  • Live Predictions: See position (LONG/SHORT/WAIT), confidence score, SL/TP, and timestamp — all updated live.
  • Continuous Learning: The system tracks and learns from every prediction, improving accuracy over time using reinforcement learning.
